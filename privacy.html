Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mama's Bill Wizard ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our software application ("Software").

2. Information We Collect

Local Application Data: Mama's Bill Wizard is a desktop application that processes and stores data locally on your computer. The Software processes XML files (CFDI) and interfaces with QuickBooks.

Authentication Information: To connect with QuickBooks, we use OAuth authentication, which requires temporary access to tokens that enable the connection between the Software and your QuickBooks account.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely for the purpose of:

  • Facilitating the connection between the Software and QuickBooks
  • Processing bill data from XML files and submitting it to QuickBooks
  • Storing authentication tokens to maintain the connection with QuickBooks

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All data processed by the Software is stored locally on your computer. We do not transmit your data to our servers.

Token Storage: Authentication tokens are stored securely in an environment file (.env) on your computer.

Data Transmission: The only data transmitted from the Software is the necessary information sent directly to the QuickBooks API to create bills.

5. Third-Party Services

The Software integrates with QuickBooks Online. Your use of QuickBooks through our Software is subject to the Intuit Privacy Statement.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your computer, you have complete control over it. You can delete the Software and all associated data at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
